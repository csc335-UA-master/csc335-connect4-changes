# CSC 335 Lab 5: Connect 4 Changes

To validate your design in the Connect 4 project, you are tasked with making two changes:

1. However you represented your board before, it must now be an array of java.lang.Boolean, with Boolean.TRUE being Red (Computer or 'O') and Boolean.FALSE being Yellow (Human or 'X') and null being a blank space. 

2. You now should be playing on a board of 8 rows and 6 columns.

Note that the model choice here is deliberately "bad" to make it unlikely you actually chose this representation of the game's state. 

Clone your project, make the changes, and check it in to your section's github classroom repository. 

This is a timed exercise, it is due by the end of section.
